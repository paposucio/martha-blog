## Greenwich Home Prices by Neighborhood

Greenwich spans a wider price range than most people expect. You can buy a condo for under $500K or a waterfront estate for $50M+. Here's how pricing breaks down by neighborhood.

### Most Affordable

| Neighborhood | Typical Range |
|---|---|
| **Byram** | $400K-$1.5M |
| **Chickahominy/Pemberwick** | $500K-$2M |
| **Cos Cob** | $700K-$4M+ |
| **Downtown (condos/co-ops)** | $500K-$5M+ |

### Mid-Range

| Neighborhood | Typical Range |
|---|---|
| **Riverside** | $1M-$6M+ |
| **Old Greenwich** | $1.2M-$8M+ |
| **Mid-Country** | $1.5M-$8M+ |

### Luxury and Ultra-Luxury

| Neighborhood | Typical Range |
|---|---|
| **Back Country** | $2M-$15M+ |
| **Belle Haven** | $5M-$50M+ |

## Prices by Property Type

- **Condos and co-ops**: $400K-$2.5M, concentrated in downtown, Byram, and Cos Cob
- **Townhomes**: $800K-$3M, mostly downtown and Old Greenwich
- **Single-family (3-4 bedrooms)**: $1M-$4M across most neighborhoods
- **Single-family (5+ bedrooms, updated)**: $3M-$8M
- **Waterfront**: $5M-$30M+ depending on location and direct water access
- **New construction**: $3M-$10M+ in Mid-Country, Old Greenwich, and Back Country

## The Most Competitive Price Band

The highest demand in Greenwich is in the **$1.5M-$4M range** for move-in-ready single-family homes. These properties in desirable school districts often receive multiple offers and sell quickly. Buyers in this range should be pre-approved and ready to act.

## What Drives Pricing

Several factors affect home values beyond just neighborhood:

- **Proximity to the train station** adds a premium, especially in Old Greenwich, Riverside, and downtown
- **School district** matters at the elementary level, as each neighborhood has its own K-5 school
- **Lot size and land** are priced at a premium, particularly in Mid-Country and Back Country
- **Condition** makes a significant difference. Move-in-ready homes command 15-25% more than comparable homes needing renovation
- **Waterfront access** carries the highest premium of all

## Entry Points for NYC Buyers

If you're relocating from Manhattan and want to keep your budget reasonable, the most common starting points are:

- **Cos Cob**: Single-family homes from $700K-$1.5M
- **Byram**: Homes starting around $500K
- **Downtown condos**: Starting around $500K-$800K

All three give you Greenwich schools and commuter rail access.
