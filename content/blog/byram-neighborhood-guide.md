## Why Byram?

Byram is Greenwich's most affordable neighborhood, and that affordability comes with a powerful advantage: full access to the Greenwich public school system, town parks, beaches, and services. For first-time buyers, investors, and families who want a Greenwich address without a seven-figure mortgage, Byram is the place to start looking.

I've worked in Greenwich real estate for over 40 years, and Byram is a neighborhood I recommend often to buyers who are smart about value. Here's what you should know.

## Greenwich Schools at Entry-Level Prices

The single biggest draw of Byram is access to **Greenwich Public Schools**, one of the top-ranked districts in Connecticut. Byram children attend:

- **New Lebanon School** (K-5)
- **Western Middle School** (6-8)
- **Greenwich High School** (9-12) — One of Connecticut's top public high schools with a comprehensive AP/IB program

This means a family buying in Byram at $500K-$700K gets the same high school education as a family in a $5M Back Country estate. That's a remarkable value proposition.

## The Community

Byram sits along the New York state border, adjacent to **Port Chester, NY**. This gives the neighborhood a distinctive character:

- A diverse, welcoming community with a mix of long-time residents and newcomers
- Strong neighborhood identity with local businesses and gathering spots
- Walking distance to Port Chester's dining scene, which has become a regional destination for restaurants
- **Byram Park** and **Byram Shore** offer public beach access, a pool, basketball courts, and green space

Byram feels different from the rest of Greenwich, and that's part of its charm. It's less manicured, more lived-in, and genuinely community-oriented.

## Investment Potential

Byram has strong fundamentals for real estate investors:

- **Rental demand** is steady, driven by young professionals and families priced out of neighboring Stamford and Port Chester
- **Price appreciation** has been consistent as buyers discover the Greenwich schools advantage
- **Multi-family properties** exist in Byram (rare elsewhere in Greenwich), offering income potential
- The neighborhood benefits from any rising tide in Greenwich's overall market

For investors, Byram offers something almost no other Greenwich neighborhood can: yield. The rent-to-price ratio is more favorable here than in any other part of town.

## Commuting from Byram

- **Port Chester Metro-North station** is within walking distance for many Byram residents
- Express trains reach Grand Central Terminal in approximately 45 minutes, making this one of the fastest commutes in Greenwich
- **I-95** is immediately accessible
- Close proximity to **Westchester County Airport (HPN)** for frequent flyers

## What Homes Cost in Byram

- **Condos and co-ops**: $400K-$600K
- **Single-family homes**: $500K-$1M
- **Larger or updated homes**: $1M-$1.5M

These are the lowest price points in Greenwich. For comparison, the median home price across all of Greenwich is approximately $1.85M. Byram offers a genuine entry point.

## Is Byram Right for You?

Byram is a strong choice if you're looking for:

- Greenwich public schools at the most affordable price point in town
- Investment properties with rental income potential
- A diverse, community-oriented neighborhood
- The fastest Metro-North commute to NYC from any Greenwich neighborhood
- An entry point to the Greenwich market with room for appreciation
