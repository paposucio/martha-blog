## Why Belle Haven?

Belle Haven is Greenwich's most exclusive residential enclave. This gated peninsula on Long Island Sound is home to some of Connecticut's most valuable real estate, with waterfront estates, private club membership, and a level of privacy that attracts high-profile buyers from around the world.

Having worked in Greenwich real estate for over 40 years, I've had the privilege of representing buyers and sellers in Belle Haven. Here's what makes this neighborhood truly one of a kind.

## The Gated Community

Belle Haven is one of the few gated residential communities in the Northeast. Key features include:

- **24/7 security** at the gatehouse entrance
- Private roads maintained by the Belle Haven community association
- A residents-only environment that ensures privacy and exclusivity
- A small, tight-knit community of approximately 100 homes

The gated nature of Belle Haven means that even casual drive-throughs don't happen here. Buyers who value privacy above all else consistently gravitate to this address.

## Waterfront Living

Belle Haven's defining feature is its position on Long Island Sound. Many homes offer:

- Direct waterfront with private docks and deep-water moorings
- Panoramic Sound views stretching to Long Island
- Protected harbor access for sailing and boating
- Sunset views that are among the best on the Connecticut coast

Waterfront estates in Belle Haven represent the pinnacle of Greenwich's residential market, and new listings generate significant attention from qualified buyers worldwide.

## Belle Haven Club

The **Belle Haven Club** is the social heart of the community, offering:

- Private beach on Long Island Sound
- Tennis courts
- Marina and sailing facilities
- Seasonal dining and social events
- A family-oriented atmosphere

Club membership is available to Belle Haven residents and adds a recreational and social dimension that extends well beyond the home itself.

## Schools Serving Belle Haven

Belle Haven families are served by:

- **Julian Curtiss School** (K-5)
- **Central Middle School** (6-8)
- **Greenwich High School** (9-12)

Many Belle Haven families also opt for Greenwich's private schools, with **Greenwich Academy**, **Brunswick School**, and **Sacred Heart Greenwich** all within a short drive.

## Commuting from Belle Haven

Despite its secluded feel, Belle Haven is well-positioned for commuters:

- **Greenwich Metro-North station** is approximately a 5-minute drive
- Express trains reach Grand Central Terminal in about 47 minutes
- Quick access to I-95 for driving to Stamford, Westchester, or the airports

## What Homes Cost in Belle Haven

Belle Haven is Greenwich's highest price tier:

- **Entry-level homes** (non-waterfront within the gates): $5M-$10M
- **Waterfront estates**: $10M-$30M
- **Premier waterfront compounds**: $30M-$50M+

Inventory in Belle Haven is extremely limited. Homes trade infrequently, and many transactions happen privately before reaching the open market. Working with an agent who has established relationships in the community is essential.

## Is Belle Haven Right for You?

Belle Haven is the right fit if you're looking for:

- The ultimate in privacy and security with gated access
- Direct waterfront living on Long Island Sound
- Private club amenities (beach, tennis, marina)
- A prestigious address in one of the Northeast's most exclusive communities
- Proximity to Greenwich's town center and Metro-North access
